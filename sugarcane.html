Bagasse → Cogeneration → Grid Power
Bagasse constitutes 28–30% of cane by weight — approximately 280–300 kg per tonne of cane, regardless of sucrose content. Combusted in a boiler coupled to a steam turbine generator, bagasse produces ~150–200 kWh per tonne of cane processed. At 10,000 ha and 70 T/ha, the mill processes 700,000 T of cane per year, yielding 196,000 T of bagasse and generating approximately 105–140 GWh of electricity annually — a continuous capacity of 12–16 MW available every milling day. Surplus power beyond mill self-consumption is exported to the grid or sold under renewable energy purchase agreements, generating a fourth revenue stream from the same land.
